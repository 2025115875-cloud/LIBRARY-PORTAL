About NAB Library

Supporting students, faculty, and researchers with digital and print resources.

Who We Are

NAB Library is a modern academic library providing access to a wide range of print and digital resources to support learning, teaching, and research.

Our Mission

To provide students and faculty with high-quality resources, services, and spaces that foster knowledge, creativity, and research excellence.

Our Vision

To be a leading academic library recognized for innovation, accessibility, and supporting lifelong learning in our community.

Features

Study rooms, digital collections, research support, workshops, printing & scanning services, and access to global databases.
